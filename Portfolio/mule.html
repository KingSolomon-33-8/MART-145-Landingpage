You're a Mule Deer!

You are a warrior! You lack the ability to think critically but make up for it in your ruggid and courageous heart. You're not the brains, you're the bronze, a dull and thick bronze. You frequent hills and draws like there is not anything prettier and aesthetically pleasing in this world. To you, it is all about frame, weight, and scars. If it is ugly, dumb, or frivolous, it does not matter to you as long as it is big and mean looking.
